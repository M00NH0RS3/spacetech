SPACE
PROBES

WHAT THEY ARE

Space probes are unmanned spacecraft designed to be sent into space for the purpose of scientific research and the collection of information about our solar system or distant celestial bodies.

TYPES OF PROBES

  • Planetary Probes
  • Interplanetary Space Probes
  • Distant Reconnaissance Probes

FIRST LAUNCH

The first space probe launched was Luna 1, a Soviet spacecraft. It was launched on January 2, 1959. Luna 1 was designed for scientific measurements in space and became the first human-made object to reach the vicinity of the Moon.
