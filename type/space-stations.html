SPACE
STATIONS

WHAT THEY ARE

Space station, an artificial structure placed in orbit and having the pressurized enclosure, power, supplies, and environmental systems necessary to support human habitation for extended periods.

TYPES OF STATIONS

  • Monolithic Stations
  • Modular Stations

FIRST LAUNCH

In the 1960s during the race to the Moon, the Soviets began to build hardware that in the 1970s became the world's first space station. On April 19, 1971, the Soviet Union launched the world’s first space station, Salyut (“Salute”).
