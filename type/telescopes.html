TELESCOPES

WHAT THEY ARE

A telescope is a tool that astronomers use to see faraway objects. Most telescopes, and all large telescopes, work by using curved mirrors to gather and focus light from the night sky.

TYPES OF TELESCOPES

  • Refractor Telescopes
  • Reflectors Telescopes
  • Catadioptrics Telescope

FIRST LAUNCH

The first space telescope was the Hubble Space Telescope. It was launched on April 24, 1990, aboard the Space Shuttle Discovery (STS-31 mission).
