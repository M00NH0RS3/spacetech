EXPLORATION
ROBOTS

WHAT THEY ARE

Exploration robots are autonomous machines designed to perform tasks on planetary surfaces and in space without direct human intervention.

TYPES OF ROBOTS

  • Autonomous space probes
  • Satellite mechanic
  • Robot dogs on the moon
  • Astronaut assistant
  • Floating space robotic assistant

FIRST LAUNCH

The first robot ever sent to space was Sputnik 1, sent by the USSR on October 4, 1957, according to NASA. Sputnik 1 was the first artificial Earth satellite and the first object created by humans to orbit Earth.
